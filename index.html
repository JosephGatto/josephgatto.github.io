Hello! My name is Joseph and I am currently a senior Ph.D. candidate at Dartmouth College in the Computer Science department. I obtained my bachelors in Computer Science from Columbia University in 2020. From 2018-2020 I was a research intern at NASA's Jet Propulsion Laboratory where I did a variety of computer vision research — ranging from Mars Rover technology to subsurface imaging. These days, I am heavily interested in studying how to build better NLP tools for clinical tasks. Specifically, I am really excited about NLP for patient portal messaging. Check out PortalPal-AI on Huggingface to see our latest work on portal messaging!
